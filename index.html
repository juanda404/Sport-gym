Hypertrophy program

Here's a hypertrophy routine divided into upper and lower body workouts. The routine can be approached in different ways depending on each individual's goals. It's important to note that everyone has their own level, so what may be considered low volume for some could be high volume for others, based on their level. It's essential to remember the principle of Individualization in training.

keep in mind

It's important that you take into account:

  • To perform specific warm-up exercises targeting the body part to be worked on that day.
  • Pay attention to the execution and technique of the exercises, prioritizing the latter.
  • If in doubt about how to perform an exercise, consult with a fitness professional.
  • Keep in mind the mind-muscle connection.
  • Apply progression.
